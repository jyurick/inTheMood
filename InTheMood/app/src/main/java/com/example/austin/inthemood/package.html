InTheMood: An app that will let you record how you felt at a moment's notice in the form of a Mood. Keeps track of all the Moods you've entered, with the ability to edit or delete past Moods as you wish. You can even, with permission, see the most recent Moods of other users of InTheMood! Filter Moods by emotional state, text, or by what's happened in the last week. You can even get a calendar view of when your Moods happened!
